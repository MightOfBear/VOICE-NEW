Psychological Counseling

Psychological counselling can help if you are unhappy with the current situation, f eel under pressure or are looking for a new way. Together we can work on solutions. You might already have a specific goal or we will develop one during the counselling.

I can guide you sympathetically through hard times, life crises and new challenges. As a psychologist, it is important to me to always stick to the ethical and scientific standards of my profession.

Which topics can you work on?

  • Develop visions and goals
  • Discover your resources and strengthen them
  • Support in difficult times (e.g. loss of a loved one, illness, career reorientation)
  • Get in better contact with your emotions
  • Work with the inner child
  • Recognizing and expressing needs
  • Self-acceptance, self-love
  • Stage fright

    • Psychological counselling does not treat or diagnose mental disorders, as psychotherapy does. It takes effect earlier, before a disorder even occurs, and is therefore a preventive and supportive method.

    It is suitable for all people who want to work on themselves or are going through a difficult phase in their life, be it personal, family-related or professional.
