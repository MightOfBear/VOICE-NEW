Psychological counseling & coaching

Are you dissatisfied with the current situation? Are you looking for a new way? Perhaps you would also like to get more in touch with your emotions or work towards a very specific goal? Then take a look at my offers for psychological counseling and coaching.

Psychological Counseling

I will guide you sensitively and thoughtfully through difficult times, life crises and new challenges. As a prospective psychologist (master's degree in summer 2022), I can support you with the necessary specialist knowledge in a competent and sensitive manner to bring your life back into balance and to work through these times as best as possible.

Psychological counseling is not intended to treat or diagnose mental disorders, as psychotherapy does. It takes effect earlier, before a disorder even occurs, and is therefore a preventive and supportive method.

It is suitable for all people who are going through a difficult phase in their life, be it personal, family or professional.

Coaching

Do you have a vision, a goal or would you like to develop one?
For people who are willing to try new things and enjoy coming up with creative solutions, coaching is a wonderful way to get the best out of them.
I'll show you how to strengthen your strengths, promote your resources, use them in a targeted manner and become the best version of yourself.
Achieve goals, successfully overcome challenges and make better use of your everyday life in order to achieve top performance.
